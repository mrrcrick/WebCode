The Policy Centre for African Peoples (PCAP)

was founded in the UK in 2008 to provide

African and other communities with avenues for improving their lives through education.
EDUCATION
We deliver workshops, masterclasses and other activities that enable our clients to use education as a tool for personal and collective improvement
LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT
We provide our service users with the training and toolkits they need to become effective leaders and change-makers.
SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT
We train, nurture and encourage people who participate in our programmes to become agents of positive social change.
